Community

Welcome to our Community page

This is where we post content about meetups and courses for members, and also where we publish a backlog of educational articles from our monthly newsletters. Members can get access to information about teacations and tea retreats offered by various providers and business members. Members can also communicate with each other and post about their own events on our forum. We sometimes hold online events or courses for members online and while we are primarily an online community, you can also get in touch with us locally at the address below where we occasionally hold face-to-face meetups in Dublin. Joining our community is quick and easy, just fill in your details in the form below and we'll send you a confirmation email. If you want to expand your knowledge with our monthly articles to learn more about tea varieties, terroirs and seasonal pickings, then please also subscribe to our newsletter!
